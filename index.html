About the game

The Counter-Strike series is a popular team-based first-person shooter video game series originating as a modification of Valve's first-person shooter Half-Life.

The gameplay consists of team of Counter-Terrorists against a team of Terrorists in rounds of competition won either by completing an objective or completely eliminating the opposing team.

Games main objective

The match consists of 8 rounds the team that wins most of the rounds wins the match, for both teams a specific mission is
assigned to perform succesfully, it's either to elimate the opposing team or another main mission
that differs depending on the team you're playing as.

  • Counter-Terrorists

    Commonly reffered to as CT the Counter-Terrorists teams main objective is to prevent the Terriorist team from planting their C4 bomb by eliminating the team, once planted they have to elimate the Terriorist team and defuse the bomb before its timer ends and blows up.

  • Terrorists

    Reffered to as T the Terrorists teams main objective is to plant the C4 bomb in a specified plant location depending on the map (A or B) and stall the Counter-Terrorists until the C4 explodes, if the elimnate all of the Counter-Terrorists
    before the timer finishes then they win the round.

Games influence

The game had a huge influence on the gaming community from an economy and trend persprective, leading to it being from one of the main games being broadcasted in popular Esports events, also being broadcasted on popular streaming platforms by popular streamers.
